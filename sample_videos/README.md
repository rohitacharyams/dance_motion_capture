# Place for sample videos
Place your dance video files here (MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV formats).

## Tips for Good Results:
- Ensure full body is visible in frame
- Good lighting conditions
- Clear, uncluttered background
- Stable camera position
- 2-3 meters distance from camera
- 30 FPS or higher

## Example Usage:
```powershell
python extract_pose.py -i sample_videos/my_dance.mp4 -o output/my_dance_motion.json
```
